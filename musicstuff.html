Music

With over 10 years of Ableton experience, I always still find myself hungry for more knowledge, which in turn extends the bounds of my creative process. My project "Envi" contains all original music, occasionally in collaboration with other artists, as well as live recordings of several other instruments including guitar and percussion. As well as containing all original sounds, the music that I have been creating recently has been accompanied by original motion graphics created in Blender 3D. The beauty of these two mediums melding is truly a love letter to creativity.
